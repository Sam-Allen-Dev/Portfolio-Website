GMTK Global Game Jam 2021 Reflection


Description

The Game Maker's Toolkit Global Game Jam, is a "48 hour game making marathon, focused on design, mechanics, and clever ideas". Tasking designers, developers and artists to create a game based on a theme within 48 hours. The theme for 2021's jam was Joined Together, and saw a total of 21,967 participants with 5,767 entries and over 142 thousand ratings globally.
